Instructions

To win the game, you have to fulfill the following requirements:

  • You have to buy all upgrades from the third upgrade slot so that the "Win"-Field appears.
  • To activate it, all nine fields need to have a grown carrot planted on them at the same time.
  • After that, you have to click on the "Win"-Field.

There are nine fields where you can grow carrots, to do so you have to unlock (buy) them first. This costs a specific number of coins depending on the field (left to right, top to bottom: 1, 10, 100, 1.000, 10.000, 100.000, 1.000.000, 10.000.000 and 100.000.000 coins).

After unlocking a field you can plant a seed for three coins which slowly grows into a carrot. As soon as the carrot is fully grown, you can harvest it by clicking on the field. By clicking on the Sell-Button (the one below the Carrot and Coin counter), you can sell all your carrots at once for five coins each.

The lootbox below the Sell-Button can be clicked every few seconds and gives you a random number of coins or carrots between 0 and 9.

Upgrades

Below the nine carrot fields, there are three upgrade slots:

  • The first upgrade increases the sell value of carrots.
  • The second upgrade increases the amount of loot you gain from the lootbox.
  • The third upgrade consists of unique upgrades which can only be bought in a limited amount. After you bought the final upgrade from this slot, the "Win"-Field will appear here.

Unique Upgrades (Spoilers!)

  • Watering Can: Allows you to speed up the growth of carrots by clicking on them. For each click the carrot grows by one stage.
  • Lootbox Cool-down: Reduces the cooldown of the lootbox drastically.
  • CarrotUp: Increases the number of carrots that grow on a field by 2. This also works for fields where carrots are already growing!
