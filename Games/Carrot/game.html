Instructions

To win the game you have to fulfill the following requirements:
* You have to buy all upgrades from the third upgrade slot so that the "Win"-Field appears.
* To activate it, all 9 fields need to have a grown carrot plantet on them at the same time.
* After that, you just have to click on the "Win"-Field.

There are 9 fields where you can grow carrots, to do so you have to unlock (buy) them first. This costs a specific amount of coins depending on the field (left to right, top to bottom: 1, 10, 100, 1.000, 10.000, 100.000, 1.000.000, 10.000.000 and 100.000.000 coins).

After unlocking a field you can plant a seed for 3 coins which slowly grows into a carrot. As soon as the carrot is fully grown you can harvest it by clicking on the field. By clicking on the Sell-Button (the one below the Carrot and Coin counter) you can sell all your carrots at once for 5 coins each.

The lootbox below the Sell-Button can be clicked every few seconds and gives you a random amount of coins or carrots between 0 and 9

Below the 9 carrot fields there are 3 upgrade slots:
* The first upgrade increases the sell value of carrots.
* The second upgrade increases the amount of loot you gain from the lootbox.
* The third upgrade consists of unique upgrades which can only be bought in a limited amount. After you bought the final upgrade from this slot the "Win"-Field will appear here.

The following unique upgrades are available (Spoilers ahead!):
* Watering Can: Allowes you to speed up the growth of carrots by clicking on them. For each click the carrot grows by one stage.
* Lootbox Cool-down: Reduces the cooldown of the lootbox drastically.
* CarrotUp: Increases the amount of carrots that grow on a field by 2. This also works for fields where carrots are already growing!
